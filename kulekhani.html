The Kulekhani Rervoir
Brihaspati Vidyasadan

From the reservoir, water is sent to the Kulekhani Hydropower Station via a 5.8km headrace tunnel to a gate house which controls the flow of water to the power station. From the gate house water travels down a 1,340m long penstock where it reaches the underground power station. It contains two 30 MW Pelton turbine-generators. The difference in elevation between the reservoir and the power station affords a net hydraulic head of 550m. i have visited this reservoir many times and it's a beautiful view.
