The Famous Rara Taal
Brihaspati Vidyasadan

Rara Lake also known as Mahendra Lake is the largest fresh water lake in the Nepalese Himalayas. It is located in Jumla and Mugu Districts of Karnali Province. Rara National Park stretches over 412 miles. It lies at an elevation of 2,990 m (9,810 ft), has a water surface of 10.8 km2 (4.2 sq mi), a maximum depth of 167 m (548 ft), is 5.1 km (3.2 mi) long and 2.7 km (1.7 mi) wide.
